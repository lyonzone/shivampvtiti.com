About US

SHIVAM INDUSTRIAL TRAINING CENTER (SITC) is a Training Center affiliated to N.C.V.T. & D.G.E.&T; This Institute has been providing Craftsmen training in Fitter and Electrician Trade since 2007. Being a Non Profits seeking institution provides high skilled training in accordance to the syllabus provided byN.C.V.T. & D.G.E.&T;,New Delhi, Ministry of labour, Government of India.
